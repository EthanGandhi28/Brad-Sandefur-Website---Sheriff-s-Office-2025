NAMI – National Alliance on Mental Illness

NAMI is one of the nation’s largest grassroots mental health organizations dedicated to building better lives for the millions of Americans affected by mental illness. They offer education, support, advocacy, and public awareness so that all individuals and families affected by mental health conditions can build better lives.

Email: info@nami.org
